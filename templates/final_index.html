About this project. Peter, Conor, and Octavio built this as their CS32 final project to make a tangible Investment tool. Each run draws monthly returns from a normal distribution calibrated to long-run historical averages, then chains 1,000 of those paths together to map out a realistic range of outcomes. The percentile bands (p10, p50, p90) tell you what the bottom 10%, middle, and top 10% of futures look like.
  • Asset mix presets use rough historical mean / volatility for each blend.
  • Strategies let you compare flat contributions vs raises (growing) vs putting it all in upfront (lump sum).
  • Impact analysis reruns the first scenario with a small tweak so you can see what actually moves the needle.
Not financial advice — just a study tool. Real markets aren't normally distributed and past returns don't guarantee future ones.
